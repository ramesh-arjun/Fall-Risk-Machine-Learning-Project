The description of a data record of the .csv file in the Participants_Information folder.

ID			: ID of participant (ps01 to ps34)
Age			: Age of participant
Sex			: Sex of participant (Male / Female)
Weight			: Weight of participant (kg)
Height			: Height of participant (cm)
Education_Level		: Education level of participant (High School - PhD)
Exercise_Behavior	: Number of days in the week that participants exercised
Smoking_Behavior	: Participants had smoking habits (0 = No / 1 = Yes)
UD_HighBloodPress	: Participant's history of Hypertension (0 = No / 1 = Yes)
UD_HighCholesterol	: Participant's history of Dyslipidemia (0 = No / 1 = Yes)
UD_Diabetes		: Participant's history of Diabetes (0 = No / 1 = Yes)
UD_MuscleWeakness	: Participant's history of Myasthenia Gravis (0 = No / 1 = Yes)
UD_Stroke		: Participant's history of Stroke (0 = No / 1 = Yes)
UD_CoronaryArtery	: Participant's history of Coronary Artery disease (0 = No / 1 = Yes)
UD_AtrialFibrillation	: Participant's history of Atrial Fibrillation disease (0 = No / 1 = Yes)
UD_PeripheralArtery	: Participant's history of Peripheral Artery disease (0 = No / 1 = Yes)
UD_HeartDisease		: Participant's history of Heart disease (0 = No / 1 = Yes)
UD_KidneyDisease	: Participant's history of Kidney disease (0 = No / 1 = Yes)
Depression		: Participant's history of Depression (0 = No / 1 = Yes)
BADL			: Participants's BADL score
MOCA_Total		: Participants's MOCA score
MOCA_Risk		: Interpretation of MOCA score (0 = Non-risk / 1 = Risk)
Fall_History		: Participant's history of falls in the past year (0 = No / 1 = Yes)
Specialist 1 		: Observation result from first specialist during participant perform TUG
Specialist 2 		: Observation result from second specialist during participant perform TUG
Specialist 3 		: Observation result from third specialist during participant perform TUG
Specialist_Conclusion	: The average of observation result from three specialist
TUG_Risk		: TUG result of participant
Time R1 (sec.)		: Duration of participant take the first round of the TUG test
Time R2 (sec.)		: Duration of participant take the second round of the TUG test
Time R3 (sec.)		: Duration of participant take the third round of the TUG test
Average (sec.)		: The average of TUG test duration
